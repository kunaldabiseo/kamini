1. Eligibility

Because Kamini products are classical Ayurvedic medicine, we follow stricter standards than general consumer goods. Returns are accepted only in the following situations:

  • The product was received damaged or tampered with (intimate us within 48 hours of delivery with a video).
  • You received the wrong product versus what you ordered.
  • The product is past expiry when shipped to you.
  • The Kamini outer carton is unopened and the foil seal is intact, and the return is initiated within 7 days of delivery.

2. Items we cannot accept back

  • Opened or partially-used Kamini packs (medicine, once opened, cannot be re-sold for safety reasons).
  • Products purchased on clearance sale or marked "final sale".
  • Items not returned within the 7-day window from delivery.
  • Products without their original outer packaging and batch label.

3. How to start a return

  1. Email care@safensure-india.com with your order number, product name and the reason for return.
  2. For damaged-in-transit returns, include an unboxing video as taken from the moment the parcel arrived.
  3. Our team will respond within one working day with a Return Authorisation (RA) number and a pick-up arrangement (in serviceable pin codes) or a self-ship address.
  4. Pack the item in its original outer carton with the RA number written on it.
  5. Once received and inspected at Bengaluru, we initiate refund or replacement within 3 working days.

4. Refunds

Refund methods follow the original payment method:

  • UPI / Net banking / Cards: 5 – 7 working days to reflect.
  • Razorpay wallet: within 24 hours.
  • COD orders: NEFT to a bank account you provide; 5 – 10 working days.

The original shipping fee (if any) is non-refundable, except when the return is due to our error.

5. Replacements

For damaged, defective or wrong-item issues, we always prefer to ship a free replacement rather than refund — most customers want the medicine. Replacement orders are dispatched within 2 working days of receiving the returned item.

6. Order cancellations

You may cancel an order any time before it has been dispatched, free of charge — write to care@safensure-india.com or WhatsApp +91 82902 68414 with your order number. Once an order is handed to the courier, it can no longer be cancelled and must be returned per the process above.
