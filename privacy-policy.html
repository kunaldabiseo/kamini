1. Introduction

Safe & Sure (India) ("we", "our", "us") operates this website and the Kamini brand of Ayurvedic proprietary medicine. This Privacy Policy explains what personal information we collect from you when you visit safensure-india.com or place an order, how we use it, and the rights you have over it. By using our website you agree to this policy.

2. Information we collect

2.1 Information you give us

  • Contact details — name, email address, phone number, shipping and billing address.
  • Order details — products purchased, quantity, payment method, order date.
  • Account details — preferences, wishlist, communication settings.
  • Optional health context shared in a consultation booking (treated with strict confidentiality).

2.2 Information collected automatically

  • Device and browser information (IP address, user-agent, screen size).
  • Pages viewed, time on site, referral source.
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies (see Section 5).

3. How we use your information

  • To process and ship your orders.
  • To send order confirmations, shipping updates and receipts.
  • To respond to enquiries, requests and complaints.
  • To improve the website and our products.
  • To send you marketing communications, only if you have opted in.
  • To comply with applicable law (including AYUSH and tax regulations).

4. Sharing & disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with trusted partners necessary to fulfil your order or operate our business:

  • Payment gateways (Razorpay, etc.) — for processing payments.
  • Shipping partners (Delhivery, India Post, Bluedart) — for delivering your order.
  • Email and WhatsApp providers — for order updates.
  • Authorities — only when required by Indian law.

5. Cookies

We use cookies and local storage to keep you logged in, remember items in your bag and wishlist, measure traffic patterns, and personalise the shopping experience. You can disable cookies in your browser, but parts of the website may not function correctly.

6. Your rights

Under applicable Indian data protection laws, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct or update inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal exceptions.
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time.
  • Lodge a complaint with the relevant data protection authority.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at care@safensure-india.com.

7. Data retention

We retain order and tax records for the period required under Indian tax and AYUSH law (typically eight years). Marketing data is held only while your consent is active. Account data is held while your account is open and for six months thereafter.

8. Security

We use TLS encryption on all pages, restrict employee access to personal data on a need-to-know basis, and never store full card details on our servers — payments are tokenised by our payment partner.
