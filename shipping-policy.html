1. Order processing

Orders placed before 2 PM IST on a working day (Monday to Saturday) are dispatched the same day from our Bengaluru warehouse. Orders placed after 2 PM, on Sundays or on public holidays are dispatched on the next working day.

Every order is hand-checked, sealed in tamper-evident packaging and labelled with the Kamini batch number before dispatch.

2. Shipping charges

  • Orders over ₹1,000: Free shipping pan-India.
  • Orders below ₹1,000: Flat ₹60 shipping fee.
  • Cash on Delivery: Additional ₹30 handling fee on COD orders.
  • Express delivery (where available): ₹120 flat fee, 2-day delivery in metros.

3. Delivery timelines

Estimated delivery times after dispatch:

  • Metros (Bengaluru, Mumbai, Delhi, Chennai, Hyderabad, Kolkata, Pune): 2 – 4 working days.
  • Tier-2 cities & state capitals: 3 – 6 working days.
  • Tier-3 cities, smaller towns & rural pin codes: 5 – 9 working days.
  • North-Eastern states, Jammu & Kashmir, Andaman & Nicobar: 7 – 12 working days.

Timelines are indicative. Delivery may be slightly longer during monsoons, festival weeks or in case of courier disruptions outside our control.

4. Tracking your order

You will receive an SMS, WhatsApp and email with your tracking link as soon as the order is picked up by our courier partner. You can also track your orders inside My Account at any time.

Our courier partners include Delhivery, Bluedart, India Post (Speed Post), Ekart and Xpressbees, selected based on serviceability and reliability for your pin code.

5. Cash on Delivery

COD is available for orders up to ₹5,000, pan-India where our courier partner services it. The COD handling fee (₹30) is added at checkout.

To reduce fraud, COD orders may be subject to a one-time OTP verification call from our team before dispatch on orders above ₹2,500.

6. International orders

We currently ship within India only. For international enquiries (USA, UK, UAE, Singapore, Canada, Australia), please email care@safensure-india.com with the product list and shipping address — we'll respond with a custom quote.

7. Delivery issues

Damaged or tampered packaging

Please record a video while opening the parcel. If a Kamini pack is damaged or the outer carton appears tampered with, refuse the delivery if possible, and contact us within 48 hours with the video. We will arrange a full replacement at no extra cost.

Lost in transit

If a shipment is marked "delivered" but you have not received it, contact us within 7 days. We file a tracer with the courier and either re-dispatch or refund based on the outcome.

Failed delivery / return to origin

If a parcel is returned to us because of incorrect address or repeated non-acceptance, re-shipping will be charged separately.
